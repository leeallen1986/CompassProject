# CDR Dryer Flyer Analysis

## Product: CDR 850 / CDR 1200 / CDR 1700
- Portable desiccant dryers for compressed air treatment
- Delivers -40°C pressure dew point on location
- Prevents moisture damage, protects equipment & processes, reduces downtime

## Three Models:
| Spec | CDR 850 | CDR 1200 | CDR 1700 |
|------|---------|----------|----------|
| Volume @ 7 bar | 880 cfm | 1,200 cfm | 1,700 cfm |
| Volume @ 14 bar | 1,430 cfm | 1,944 cfm | 2,754 cfm |
| Dew point | -40°C | -40°C | -40°C |
| Pressure range | 7-16 bar | 7-16 bar | 7-16 bar |
| Weight | 2,300 kg | 2,550 kg | 3,500 kg |

## Key Advantages:
- -40°C consistent dew point (up to 20% extra desiccant for harsh conditions)
- Fully pneumatic — no external power needed
- Compact & mobile — heavy-duty frame with lifting lugs and forklift slots
- All filters included — water separator, oil-coalescing & outlet filter

## Target Applications (from flyer):
1. **Mining & Exploration** — extreme heat, dust, remote conditions demand reliable dry air for pneumatic tools and drilling
2. **Chemical & Process** — production instrumentation and process air require consistent moisture-free supply
3. **Oil & Gas** — protect critical instrumentation and pneumatic controls from moisture damage
4. **Rental Fleets** — rental companies
5. **Pipeline Maintenance** — pipeline testing and maintenance
