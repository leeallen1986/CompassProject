# Y1260 DrillAir Flyer — Key Notes

## Product Identity
- **DrillAir Y1260** — High-Pressure Air Built for Serious Drilling
- Power Technique — DrillAir Range
- 35 bar max pressure (507 PSI), 1,382 cfm max flow
- 975L fuel tank, 97 L/hr @ 100% load, 81 dBA @ 7 metres
- 5.1 metres — fits on one truck with drill rig

## Target Applications (from flyer)
1. Water Well Drilling
2. Geothermal Drilling
3. Foundation Drilling
4. High-Pressure DTH (Down-The-Hole)
5. Multi-Application Fleets

## Key Selling Points
- DrillAirXpert: Simple pressure & flow control from control panel
- Dynamic Flow Boost: Faster flushing & drill stem refill
- Aftercooler: For demanding high-pressure drilling conditions
- 5.1M portable design: Compressor + drill rig fit on one truck
- Drill faster and deeper at 21-35 bar range
- Higher drilling speed improves revenue (more metres drilled per hour)
- Lower cost per metre

## Target Buyer Profile
- "Built for Contractors Who Want Control of Productivity"
- Own the asset — control availability
- Standardise fleet performance
- One high-pressure platform across varied drilling jobs
- Drive lower cost per metre over time
- Strong resale value within business model

## Matching Strategy
Unlike XAVS1800 (blasting) or CDR (air treatment), the Y1260 is specifically for:
- **Drilling contractors** running RC, DTH, water well, geothermal, foundation drilling
- **Fleet owners** who buy (not rent) and care about cost per metre, uptime, resale
- **Mine-site drilling support** where pressure and flow matter

Key differences from X1350:
- X1350 is 25 bar / 1,350 cfm — more general purpose RC drilling
- Y1260 is 35 bar / 1,382 cfm — high-pressure specialist (water well, geothermal, foundation, DTH)
- Y1260 targets owner-operators and fleet standardisation
- Y1260 is about drilling productivity and cost per metre
