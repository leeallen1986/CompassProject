# Portable Air Full Potential Scope

This module represents the Portable Air Full Potential operating model.

The account universe, import workbook, route-to-market rules, actions and My Week action rhythm are intended for Portable Air / APE GTM execution.

## Important clarification

Some users may sit within the broader Power Technique team, and some accounts may involve wider Power Technique relationships. That does not change the scope of this module.

The module should be described in product copy and operating language as:

```text
Portable Air Full Potential
```

or, where space is limited:

```text
Portable Air FP
```

## What this module is

- Portable Air account universe.
- Portable Air route-to-market and channel logic.
- Portable Air target/account action tracking.
- Portable Air weekly action rhythm.
- Admin-controlled Portable Air workbook import.

## What this module is not

- Not a full Power Technique account universe.
- Not a C4C replacement.
- Not a C4C write-back integration.
- Not a general CRM clean-up project.
- Not a cross-business-line Full Potential model unless explicitly extended later.

## C4C note

C4C integration is intentionally out of scope until the platform proves useful and API approval is justified. Full Potential actions should remain internal Compass operating actions for now.
